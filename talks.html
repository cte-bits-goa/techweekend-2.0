Curvature Constrained Path Planning

by Mohit Gupta

Motion planning is a fundamental research area in robotics. Numerous methods have been proposed to accomplish this task. However, the motion of car-like vehicles is constrained by their minimum turning radius, making it necessary to be considered during planning paths for such vehicles. We will briefly discuss the concept of continuity and visit many such algorithms starting from the very primitive to continuous curvature path planning techniques.
