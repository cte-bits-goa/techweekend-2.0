BITS & Bids: The Maker's Auction

Electronic and Robotics Club (ERC)

Gear up for an electrifying robotics experience with our one-of-a-kind home automation auction challenge! In this event, participant teams will bid on essential robotics components, such as sensors, microcontrollers, and actuators, to assemble a functional home automation system. Each team will receive a virtual budget and must strategically plan their purchases during the auction to outsmart their competitors. Teams will design innovative solutions using these parts to solve given problem statements within a limited time frame. Adding a twist, we’ve introduced a “blind auction” for priority-priced items, where teams must weigh their risks and rewards carefully. There’s also a black market trade for teams to barter and maximize their resources creatively!
